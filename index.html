Elon Reeve Musk

The man pioneering the transition of our society to sustainable energy and a new age of space exploration

About Elon Reeve Musk:

  • Born in Pretoria, Transvaal, South Africa in 1971
  • Moved to Canada at age 17
  • Holds two bachelor's degrees in economics and physics from the University of Pennsylvania
  • Moved to California in 1995 where he co-founded web software company Zip2 which was later acquired by Compaq in 1999 for US $307 million
  • Founded SpaceX in 2002 and joined Tesla Motors in 2004
  • SpaceX achieved the first vertical soft landing of a reusable orbital rocket stage on December 21, 2015, after delivering 11 Orbcomm OG-2 commercial satellites into low Earth orbit. The first reuse of a Falcon 9 first stage occurred on 30 March 2017
  • Tesla was ranked as the best-selling electric vehicle manufacturer worldwide after selling close to 421,000 units in the first half of 2021. Tesla's sales volume translates into a market share of about 15 percent
  • Has a goal of establishing a permanent human presence on Mars
